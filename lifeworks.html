Introduction
LifeWorks is a global leader in employee management and wellbeing solutions. Through its product offerings, it seeks to elevate "total wellbeing" - that is, mental, financial, social, and physical wellbeing.

My role
As a UX designer, I work on agile product teams to continuously introduce new features and improve existing UX/UI.

My responsibilities include:
  • Producing wireframes and high-fidelity mockups for websites and native mobile applications
  • Preparing images/assets for development team
  • Create high fidelity prototypes for client demos
  • Assessing user needs
  • Maintaining company branding standards by working within a design system

My SSP
On this portfolio you'll find many references to My SSP. What is it, exactly?

My Student Support Program (My SSP) is a desktop and mobile platform where high school and university students can access professional counseling as well as an extensive library of digital emotional health and wellbeing content. Since joining LifeWorks, this has been my main, ongoing project, for which I'm the sole designer on the team. Due to the ever-evolving nature of the product, please forgive the small discrepancies (e.g.: differences in the page headers, button styles, etc) between the mockups presented in this portfolio.
