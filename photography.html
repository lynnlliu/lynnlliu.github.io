Photography
I love brainstorming and executing photoshoots built around unifying characteristics such as mood, colour, and literary/cinematic inspirations.

Photo collection: U of T
During my final year of university, I sought to capture the timelessness and nostalgia of U of T with some vintage-inspired photos. Drawing inspiration from films such as An Education (2009) and Kill Your Darlings (2013), it was fun to reimagine the places I pass by every day as the setting of a dramatic school story of decades past.
